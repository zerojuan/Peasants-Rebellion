VS

The Peasants are Revolting!

Peasant's Rebellion is a game about a king, his peasants and a rebellion staged on a chess board.

It's a multiplayer take on the classic chess variant: Peasant's Revolt. Inspired by Kasparov vs the World, players can play as the King and defend against a mob of Peasants (random players on the internet who happened to click a link to your game).

To get started, "sign" either of the forms below:
